ADMISSION REQUIREMENTS

The prerequisites for each of the programs.

Please review the required admission requirements for our programs.

To register for the competition:
For 1st year:

Hold a scientific, economic, or technical baccalaureate.


For 2nd year:

Hold a certificate of success in the 1st year of preparatory classes or a certificate of success in the 1st year of an engineering school (integrated preparatory classes).


For 3rd year:

Hold a scientific degree or be admitted to the national engineering competition.


For 4th year:

Hold a scientific or technical master's degree (M1) in the field of specialty.


For evening courses:

Hold a scientific or technical bachelor's degree (4 years of study).
