ONLINE_TRAINING

Esprit, when it was created, gave itself the main mission of training engineers “differently”. For this, the school adopted from the outset a new, dynamic pedagogy putting the student at the center of learning. The adoption of APP (learning by project/problem) has reinforced this desire. A pedagogical innovation center allows teachers to be trained in these new pedagogical practices, which are gradually being extended to the entire course. The evaluation of teaching by the students also contributes to putting the latter at the heart of the learning process. The student is an actor in his training.
